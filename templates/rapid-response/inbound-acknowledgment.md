# Inbound Acknowledgment + Discovery Questions

**Use when:** A new solicitation, RFP, or inquiry arrives and needs a first touch within 3-5 minutes.

---

## Acknowledgment (send immediately)

Subject: RE: [Solicitation Title / Inquiry]

Thank you for reaching out. We've received your [solicitation/inquiry/request] and are reviewing it now.

To ensure we provide the most relevant response, a few quick questions:

1. What is the target completion date or contract start date?
2. Is there a set-aside or preference for small/minority-owned businesses?
3. Are there any mandatory pre-qualification requirements we should be aware of?
4. Who is the best point of contact for follow-up questions?

We'll have a substantive response back to you within [24/48] hours.

## Discovery Questions (attach to intake record)

- What is the estimated contract value range?
- Is this a new award or re-compete? Who is the incumbent?
- Are there bonding/insurance requirements beyond standard GL?
- Is subcontracting permitted? Required?
- What NAICS codes are listed?
- What is the evaluation criteria weighting (price vs. technical)?
